Millet Mohan: Embracing Sustainable Farming

A powerful and evocative stage play, Rishi Das' original work, "Millet Mohan." Set in pre-independence India, the play takes the audience on a journey from the past to the present, emphasizing the importance of preserving traditional agricultural practices and embracing sustainable farming for a better future. The play's timeless message is not only relevant to farmers but also resonates with audiences from all walks of life, encouraging a deeper understanding of the challenges faced by the agricultural sector today. "Millet Mohan" serves as an educational tool, showcasing the potential of traditional crops and sustainable farming practices to address contemporary ecological and socio-economic challenges. The play also makes sure to mention the various government initiatives already in place to help farmers in India, directing the audience to where they can find these resources, thereby providing practical support for farmers and their communities. By integrating this production into school curriculums across India, students will develop a profound appreciation for their cultural heritage and the wisdom of their ancestors whilst also giving the students an opportunity to showcase their talents on stage. This play will inspire students to become advocates for a sustainable future, equipped with the knowledge and passion to contribute to the preservation and revitalization of India's rich agricultural traditions.
